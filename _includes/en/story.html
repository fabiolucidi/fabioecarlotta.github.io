They say we first met in the year 2000. Or maybe it was 2001 or 2002...

It doesn’t really matter, neither of us truly remembers. What matters is that twenty years later, in Milan, everything changed.


From that moment on, things slowly began to take shape: concerts that turned into habits, spontaneous trips and carefully planned ones, departures, returns, new departures and that growing feeling of being in the right place, together.

And almost without realizing it, we too found a place that became dear to our hearts, where over the years we came together for dinners, Ferragosto celebrations, aperitifs, graduation parties, and birthdays.


For this reason, on the twentieth of June, two thousand and twenty-six, we will return once again to Villa Pellegrini—but this time to celebrate our wedding and to write the rest of our story together with all of you.
